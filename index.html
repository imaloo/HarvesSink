02 — The Solution

Source-Level Intelligence for Urban Water.

HarvesSink sits under your kitchen sink. It reads the water, decides its fate, and acts — in under 10 seconds. No plumber needed. No daily interaction. Just silent, autonomous protection.

A dual-layer "Reflex + Brain" architecture ensures 100% uptime: edge logic handles real-time actuation, while the ML brain predicts invisible contaminants and connects to the city grid.

04 — ML Innovation

Predicting what labs can't measure at-sink.

BOD (Biological Oxygen Demand), COD (Chemical Oxygen Demand), and Ammonia are the gold-standard metrics for water toxicity. Normally they require lab equipment and 5-day incubation. We predict them in real-time from ₹500 sensors.

Our XGBoost Multi-Output Regressor — trained on 1,300+ days of Bengaluru Sewage Treatment Plant operational data — performs "Soft-Sensing": mapping cheap analog readings to lab-grade outputs with location-aware one-hot encoding for different urban industrial profiles.

05 — Safety Engineering

Quad-Guard™ — 4-Tier Watchdog

FMEA-validated. 37 failure modes. Default-to-drain. Every single time.

01
Boundary Check
Catastrophic hardware failure detection. pH < 1 or pH > 13 triggers immediate isolation. Catches sensor wire breaks, electrode failure.
Hardware Fault
02
Signal Integrity
Zero-variance detection identifies "stuck" or frozen sensors. A flat signal = failed sensor = drain immediately. No silent failures.
Signal Health
03
Local Z-Score Nudge
Edge learns household tap water baseline. Deviations beyond 4.5σ flag sudden chemical anomalies — soap splash, cleaning product — with 3s debounce.
Statistical
04
Correlation Physics
"Impossible physics" detection: high turbidity + low TDS is physically inconsistent. Signals sensor cross-contamination or maintenance needed. Drains.
Physics Check
